Bank credit to NBFCs nearly 4× the FY25 incremental in a single year

₹20.66L Cr (+26.3% FY-end) — FY26 incremental ₹4.30L Cr vs ₹1.12L Cr in FY25

Bank credit to NBFCs closed FY26 at ₹20.66L Cr, up from ₹16.35L Cr in Mar 2025 — a 26.3% FY move and nearly 4× the FY25 incremental. The 2023 risk-weight tightening cycle that compressed NBFC funding through FY25 is fully absorbed.

The NBFC funding channel is fully open. Fintechs and NBFCs with bank co-lending agreements are now running on the system's fastest-growing funding source. The risk: RBI could re-tighten risk weights if it perceives NBFC overheating — watch H1 FY27 FSR.
