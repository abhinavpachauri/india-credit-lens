Gold loans: the structural shift from unsecured to secured household borrowing

+359% over 24 months — Mar 2024, Mar 2025, Feb 2026 all confirming
  ▲ Stronger than January — now confirmed across 3 data points

RBI's November 2023 risk-weight increase on unsecured lending made credit cards more capital-expensive. That demand didn't disappear — it migrated to gold loans, which are secured, RBI-friendly, and competitively priced.

⚠ Note: the volume shift is confirmed data. The demand migration path (credit cards → gold loans) is a working hypothesis — not independently sourced.
